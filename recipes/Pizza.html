Pizza Recipe

Pizza is one of the most popular and versatile foods in the world. It starts with a simple base of dough that’s topped with rich tomato sauce, melted cheese, and any number of toppings, from classic pepperoni to fresh vegetables. The combination of a crisp crust, gooey cheese, and flavorful toppings makes it a comfort food favorite. Homemade pizza is especially rewarding because you can customize it to your taste. Whether you like thin and crunchy or thick and fluffy crust, a light layer of toppings or a fully loaded pie, pizza is all about creating something delicious that brings people together.

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour (plus extra for dusting)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 packet (2 ¼ tsp) active dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm water (around 110°F / 45°C)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Toppings (Personal Preference)

  • ½ cup pizza sauce (tomato-based)
  • 1–2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Anything else that you like
  1. Prepare the dough:
    • In a small bowl, dissolve the yeast and sugar in warm water. Let it sit for 5–10 minutes until it becomes frothy.
    • In a large bowl, combine the flour and salt. Make a well in the center and pour in the yeast mixture and olive oil.
    • Mix until it forms a dough.
  2. Knead the dough:
    • Turn the dough onto a floured surface and knead for about 8–10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  3. Let it rise:
    • Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover it with a towel, and let it rise in a warm place for 1–2 hours, or until it doubles in size.
  4. Preheat the oven:
      Set your oven to 475°F (245°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it inside to heat up.
  5. Shape the dough:
    • Punch down the risen dough and roll it out into your desired pizza shape and thickness on a floured surface.
    • Transfer it to a greased baking sheet or preheated pizza stone.
  6. Add toppings:
    • Spread a thin layer of pizza sauce over the dough.
    • Sprinkle shredded mozzarella evenly over the sauce.
    • Add any other toppings you like (pepperoni, veggies, herbs).
  7. Bake the pizza:
    • Bake in the preheated oven for 12–15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Serve:
    • Let the pizza cool for a couple of minutes before slicing. Enjoy hot!

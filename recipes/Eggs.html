Scrambled Eggs Recipe

Scrambled eggs are a classic breakfast favorite—soft, fluffy, and full of flavor. They’re quick to make and can be enjoyed on their own, with toast, or as part of a bigger breakfast spread. This recipe is perfect for beginners learning to cook or anyone who wants a tasty, satisfying meal in just a few minutes.

Ingredients

  • 2–4 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons milk (optional, for extra fluffiness)
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 teaspoon butter or oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, whisk the eggs with milk (if using), salt, and pepper until fully combined.
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick skillet over medium heat and melt the butter or heat the oil.
  3. Cook the eggs: Pour the eggs into the skillet. Let them sit for a few seconds, then gently stir with a spatula. Continue to fold and stir until the eggs are softly set and creamy.
  4. Serve immediately: Remove from heat before they’re fully firm (they’ll continue cooking slightly). Add any toppings you like and enjoy!

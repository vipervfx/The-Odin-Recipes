Spaghetti Recipe

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish that’s creamy, comforting, and full of flavor. It combines simple ingredients—spaghetti, eggs, cheese, and crispy bacon or pancetta—into a rich, silky sauce without using cream. This dish is perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or impressing friends with authentic Italian flavors.

  • 200g (7 oz) spaghetti
  • 100g (3.5 oz) pancetta or bacon, diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan or Pecorino cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced (optional)
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • A small handful of chopped parsley (optional, for garnish)
